welcome to HOH

Hanna Orphans Home (HOH) is an Addis Ababa-based orphanage caring for marginalized children in need of help. With branches located throughout Ethiopia, our staff cares for over 235 orphans, most of which have tragically lost their parents to the HIV/AIDS epidemic.

Our goal at HOH is to offer orphans and those in the most fragile stages of infancy and early childhood a safe harbor to heal, grow, and experience genuine love.learn more
